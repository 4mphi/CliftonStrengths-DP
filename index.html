Personal Take
These five make a lot of sense when I look at how I actually move through life. I don't do surface-level. Whether it's a friendship, a game, or something I'm trying to learn, I go all in or I don't bother. Relator and Adaptability might seem like opposites but they're not. I'm loyal to the people and things that matter, and I don't fall apart when plans change. Strategic, Analytical, and Learner all feed each other: I learn because I genuinely want to understand things, I break them down until they make sense, and then I figure out the best way forward. It's not about being the smartest person in the room. It's about actually knowing what I'm doing and why.
